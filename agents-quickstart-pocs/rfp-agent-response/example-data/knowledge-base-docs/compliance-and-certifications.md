# Compliance and Certifications

## Regulatory Compliance Framework
AnyCompany maintains comprehensive compliance programs to meet the stringent requirements of financial services regulations worldwide.

## Key Certifications
- **SOC 2 Type II**: Annual audits by Big Four accounting firm
- **PCI DSS Level 1**: Quarterly vulnerability scans and annual assessments
- **ISO 27001:2013**: Information security management system certification
- **ISO 9001:2015**: Quality management system certification
- **FedRAMP**: Authorized for moderate impact cloud services

## Regulatory Compliance
- **FFIEC**: Full compliance with examination requirements
- **SOX**: Section 404 IT controls and financial reporting
- **GDPR**: Data protection and privacy compliance for EU operations
- **CCPA**: California Consumer Privacy Act compliance
- **PSD2**: Strong Customer Authentication and Open Banking compliance

## Data Protection and Privacy
- **Encryption**: AES-256 encryption for data at rest and in transit
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
- **Data Retention**: Automated data lifecycle management
- **Privacy by Design**: Built-in privacy controls in all systems
- **Data Residency**: Compliance with local data sovereignty requirements

## Audit and Monitoring
- **Continuous Monitoring**: Real-time compliance monitoring dashboards
- **Internal Audits**: Quarterly internal compliance assessments
- **External Audits**: Annual third-party security and compliance audits
- **Penetration Testing**: Bi-annual penetration testing by certified firms
- **Vulnerability Management**: Weekly vulnerability scans and remediation

## Risk Management
- **Risk Assessment**: Annual enterprise risk assessments
- **Business Continuity**: Comprehensive disaster recovery and BCP
- **Vendor Management**: Third-party risk assessment program
- **Incident Management**: 24/7 incident response and escalation procedures
- **Change Management**: Formal change control processes
