# RiskGuard Treasury - Advanced Risk Management Platform

## Risk Management Overview
RiskGuard Treasury provides comprehensive risk management capabilities for treasury operations, covering market risk, credit risk, liquidity risk, and operational risk across all treasury activities.

## Market Risk Management

### Foreign Exchange Risk
- **Exposure Identification**: Automatic detection of FX exposures across all transactions
- **Hedge Accounting**: ASC 815/IFRS 9 compliant hedge accounting
- **Hedge Effectiveness Testing**: Prospective and retrospective testing
- **FX Derivatives**: Forward contracts, options, swaps, and exotic instruments
- **Sensitivity Analysis**: Delta, gamma, vega, and theta calculations

### Interest Rate Risk
- **Duration Analysis**: Modified duration and convexity calculations
- **Basis Point Value**: Interest rate sensitivity measurements
- **Yield Curve Analysis**: Multi-curve modeling and scenario analysis
- **Interest Rate Derivatives**: Swaps, caps, floors, and swaptions
- **Asset-Liability Matching**: Duration matching and immunization strategies

### Commodity Risk
- **Price Exposure**: Commodity price risk identification and measurement
- **Hedge Strategies**: Physical and financial hedging instruments
- **Correlation Analysis**: Cross-commodity and basis risk analysis
- **Volatility Modeling**: Historical and implied volatility analysis
- **Commodity Derivatives**: Futures, forwards, options, and swaps

## Credit Risk Management

### Counterparty Risk Assessment
- **Credit Rating Integration**: Real-time feeds from major rating agencies
- **Probability of Default**: PD modeling using market and fundamental data
- **Loss Given Default**: LGD estimation based on recovery analysis
- **Exposure at Default**: EAD calculations for various instrument types
- **Credit Valuation Adjustment**: CVA calculations for derivative portfolios

### Credit Limit Management
- **Dynamic Limit Setting**: Risk-adjusted credit limits by counterparty
- **Limit Monitoring**: Real-time exposure tracking against limits
- **Limit Utilization**: Current and projected limit usage analysis
- **Escalation Procedures**: Automated alerts for limit breaches
- **Credit Committee Workflow**: Approval processes for limit changes

### Portfolio Credit Risk
- **Concentration Analysis**: Geographic, sector, and rating concentrations
- **Correlation Modeling**: Default correlation estimation
- **Portfolio VaR**: Credit value-at-risk calculations
- **Stress Testing**: Scenario-based credit loss analysis
- **Economic Capital**: Risk-adjusted capital allocation

## Liquidity Risk Management

### Liquidity Gap Analysis
- **Maturity Bucketing**: Cash flow analysis by time periods
- **Behavioral Modeling**: Non-maturity deposit and loan modeling
- **Stress Scenarios**: Liquidity stress testing under adverse conditions
- **Survival Horizon**: Time to liquidity exhaustion analysis
- **Funding Concentration**: Analysis of funding source diversification

### Liquidity Metrics
- **Liquidity Coverage Ratio**: LCR calculation and monitoring
- **Net Stable Funding Ratio**: NSFR calculation and forecasting
- **Liquidity Risk Appetite**: Risk tolerance framework
- **Early Warning Indicators**: Proactive liquidity risk signals
- **Contingency Planning**: Liquidity crisis management procedures

- **Reverse Stress Testing**: Identification of breaking point scenarios
