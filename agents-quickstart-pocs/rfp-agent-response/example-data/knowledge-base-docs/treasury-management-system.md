# TreasuryMax Pro - Advanced Treasury Management System

## System Overview
TreasuryMax Pro is our comprehensive treasury management platform designed for corporate treasurers and financial institutions requiring sophisticated cash management, liquidity optimization, and risk management capabilities.

## Core Functionality

### Cash Management
- Real-time cash position monitoring across multiple accounts and currencies
- Automated cash concentration and sweeping
- Cash forecasting with AI-powered predictive analytics
- Multi-bank connectivity via SWIFT, API, and file-based integration
- Intraday liquidity management and optimization

### Payment Processing
- Domestic and international wire transfers
- ACH processing with same-day capabilities
- Real-time payment processing (RTP/FedNow)
- Bulk payment processing with approval workflows
- Payment fraud detection and prevention

### Investment Management
- Money market fund integration
- Certificate of deposit management
- Commercial paper and repo transactions
- Investment portfolio tracking and reporting
- Yield optimization algorithms

### Risk Management
- Foreign exchange exposure analysis
- Interest rate risk assessment
- Counterparty risk monitoring
- Value-at-Risk (VaR) calculations
- Stress testing and scenario analysis
