# CashFlow Pro - Comprehensive Cash Management Solutions

## Solution Overview
CashFlow Pro delivers advanced cash management capabilities designed to optimize liquidity, reduce operational costs, and enhance financial visibility across your organization.

## Core Cash Management Features

### Real-Time Cash Visibility
- Consolidated view of cash positions across all accounts and entities
- Multi-currency support with real-time FX conversion
- Intraday balance updates from 500+ global banks
- Historical cash flow analysis and trending
- Customizable dashboards and alerts

### Cash Concentration and Pooling
- Automated cash sweeping and concentration
- Physical and notional pooling structures
- Multi-level pooling hierarchies
- Target balance management
- Overdraft protection and optimization

### Cash Forecasting
- AI-powered predictive cash flow modeling
- Rolling 13-week cash forecasts
- Scenario planning and stress testing
- Integration with budget and planning systems
- Variance analysis and forecast accuracy tracking
